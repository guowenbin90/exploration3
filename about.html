About Me

Wenbin Guo is a Ph.D. student studying industrial engineering at the University of Missouri. His research focuses on ergonomics. He implemented various eye tracking devices such as Tobii EyeX, Hololens, Eyetech and Pupil Pro to design and develop experiments associated with human performance and behavior modeling.
